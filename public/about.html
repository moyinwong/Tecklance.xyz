What is Tecklance?

Tecklance is an online marketplace for freelance services. We aim to provide a platform for freelancers to offer services to customers worldwide

Who can use Tecklance

Anyone. We believe that everybody needs help for different tasks from time to time. We also think everyone should utilize their experience to help others

Why should I use Tecklance

If you are looking for help, Tecklance can provide you with a vast diversity of talented and experienced individuals to solve your problems. Not only cheaper, but faster and better.

If you are offering help, Tecklance is a great place for you to earn some extra money and expose to a different market where your skills could truely shine
